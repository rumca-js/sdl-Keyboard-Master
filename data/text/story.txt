[ENG]

In the year 2038 human race made a contact
with intelligent life forms from outside this galaxy.

We celebrated, optimism and joy filled the Earth.
The telescope dishes focused on a planet near Alpha Centauri star system.

In 2106 year chief of the Earch World Party Dymitrii Lukaszwili begun construction of the intergalactic trade bridge between Earch and Alpha Centauri.
Best world engineers were hired to produce the best results. The bridge was standing in early 2108.

It was a good bridge. Engineers knew what they were doing.

Plans were ambitious, but we managed to pull them off. Humanity reached the peak of our abilities. It started to worry the Alpha Centauri Unified Government.

It was 2109 year when ACUG decided to take steps that would allow their government to remain as the dominant player in game of prosperity. A new operation code named "Alphabet" was devised. It was decided to nuke Earth. For that sole purpose uncountable quantities of atomic missles were produced. Their shaped was designed to not rise any suspicitions about their nature.

That is how the battle for Earth begun.

I this neverending war humanity needed a Hero. You are the hero. You are the keyboard master - the highly trained operative able to type letters at lightning speed. You are the final defence of the Earth.

Earth future depends on you.... we count on you...
